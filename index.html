Your precious pets need medical attention? Talented and experienced veterinarians can be found on this site for the purpose of helping your loving animals! We currently do procedures on cats, dogs, parrots, and guinea pigs.


Offered Services

In the chance that your pet may try to reproduce, it is crucial to have it neutered to prevent that from happening.
Your pet will be sedated prior to the procedure and they cannot eat for at least an hour after they leave the clinic.
Cost: $100.00/hour

Teeth needing a clean or not looking normal? Do not worry! A full mouth checkup will be completed to make sure every last tooth is healthy.
Your pet cannot eat anything (can drink water) for two hours prior to coming to the clinic.
Cost: $40.00/hour

Notice a critter on your pet that will not seem to budge when you try to remove it? Use this service!
The areas in which ticks are located will be examined and action will be taken to remove every single tick from your animal in the safest way possible.
Cost: $25.00/hour

It is common to find a random bump on your pet and the severity of them may vary; however, you must immediately give your animal medical help if you believe that it has a tumor.
The areas in which tumors are located will be examined and a surgery will need to be performed as quickly and as safely as possible. Sedation is required and the animal cannot eat for three hours after leaving the clinic.
Cost: $150.00/hour

Notice that your pet is bleeding or has an open wound on their body? Come to the clinic to quickly resolve the issue!
The areas in which open wounds are located will be examined to determine whether the animal needs sedation for the procedure and then the stitching will commence.
Cost: $50.00/hour
